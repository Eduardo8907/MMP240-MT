We, "Renacer Bolivian Restaurante", opened our doors in the begining of 2010 due to popular demand of friends, family, and clients. Originally the former name was "La Cumbre". We started out just catering for special events and due to our success we were able to open our establishment at 67-03 Woodside Ave. Our goal is to keep the essence of our Bolivian culture through its diverse cuisine and music. To allow you to indulge your taste buds to our exquisite dishes. To please your ears with our folkloric songs and at the same time offer you great service and a chance to join our friendly Bolivian family. Thanks to everyone who helped us make this posible and to you for taking interest in us. We hope to see you soon.
